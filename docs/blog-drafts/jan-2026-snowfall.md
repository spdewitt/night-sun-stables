**Body:**

The first real snow of 2026 rolled into Evansville this January, and the
whole farm turned into a winter postcard. Our horses didn't seem to mind
one bit — bundled up in their turnout blankets, they were happy to stroll
around the snowy paddock and pose for a photo or two.

Winter is one of our favorite times to show off the care that goes into
every horse at Night Sun Stables. When the temperatures drop, blankets go
on, water gets checked so it never freezes over, and everyone gets extra
hay to help stay warm. Little touches — like the mini in his snowman
blanket — are what make barn life in the winter so much fun.

Snow days don't slow us down, either. With our indoor arena, lessons and
riding keep going all winter long, no matter what the weather outside is
doing.
