**Body:**

Summer camp is officially underway at Night Sun Stables, and our first
session of 2026 was one to remember! Campers spent their days learning
the ropes — literally and figuratively — with plenty of hands-on time
around our horses.

The days were packed. Campers learned to groom and care for the
horses, worked on their riding in the arena, and took on fun teamwork
challenges — including our pool-noodle-and-umbrella "spook-proofing"
game that teaches the horses to stay calm and the kids to work together.

And of course, no camp is complete without our famous horse painting!
From a rainbow-covered "Cruse" to a red-white-and-blue patriotic pony,
the kids let their creativity run wild (washable paint only — the horses
don't seem to mind a bit).

Watching new riders grow in confidence over just a few days is exactly
why we love what we do. A huge thank-you to all our campers and families
for a wonderful first session.

Want in on the fun? We still have spots open in our August 3–5 camp, and
we host camps and events all year long. Call (812) 499-3403 or message
us on Facebook to grab a spot.
